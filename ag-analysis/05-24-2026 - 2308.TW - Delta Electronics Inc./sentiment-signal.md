## Sentiment Analyst Signal Report — 2308.TW (Delta Electronics, Inc.)

**Signal:** Bullish
**Confidence:** 78 / 100

> Originally briefed as "2371.TW" but Delta trades as **2308.TW**. This report is on Delta.

### News Sentiment

The news flow over the last 30-90 days is overwhelmingly positive and concentrated around AI infrastructure.

- **NVIDIA GTC 2026 (March-May) / Computex 2026:** Delta showcased its 800V HVDC architecture jointly developed with NVIDIA, including 660 kW In-Row Power Racks with embedded battery backup and 2.4 MW In-Row liquid-cooling CDUs. NVIDIA's next-gen GB300 AI servers need far more power per rack and liquid cooling — Delta is one of the few vendors making both at NVIDIA's scale.
- **Market share:** Multiple analyst notes cite Delta as **>60% market share in AI-server power supplies** and the lead partner on the 800 VDC reference design. With the GB200/GB300 transition, 5.5 kW PSUs are replacing 3.3 kW units, doubling ASP. Delta is also rolling out 12 kW PSUs, doubling ASPs again.
- **Q1 FY2026 earnings (April 30, 2026):** Revenue NT$159.4B (+36% YoY), record gross margin **37%**, net profit +101% YoY, EPS NT$7.91. Management guided Q2 sales **+20-30% QoQ**.
- **April monthly sales:** +43.9% YoY, confirming AI momentum into Q2.
- **Thailand subsidiary (Delta Electronics Thailand, SET-listed):** Record Q1 2026 with revenue +56% YoY, profit +71% YoY; two new factories opened in Q1.
- **EV segment:** Modestly negative — EV revenue soft on slow end-market recovery, but EV is now a small share of mix vs. AI power. Not thesis-breaking.

Net news impact: **Strongly positive**, one minor negative (EV).

### Insider Activity

Delta is Taiwan-listed; US 13F / SEC Form 4 do not apply. Taiwan reports insider trades via **MOPS** (Market Observation Post System).

- Public news searches surfaced no flagged material insider selling/buying by Chairman Yancey Hai or other directors in the last 90 days.
- Founding-family entities (Cheng family, Bridge Mark) remain large long-term holders with no disclosed disposals despite the stock at all-time highs.
- **Caveat:** A direct MOPS pull was not possible with the tools available. Verify at mops.twse.com before trading.

Insider read: **Neutral to mildly constructive** — absence of insider selling into a near-6x rally is itself a mild positive.

### Institutional Positioning

- **Foreign ownership** (share of float owned by non-Taiwan investors — TWSE publishes daily): Delta is one of the most heavily foreign-owned large caps on the TWSE, historically running ~75-78%. No media reports of significant foreign net selling in May 2026.
- **Index demand:** Top-10 weight in TAIEX, meaningful weight in MSCI Taiwan / MSCI EM — passive ETF flows have been a structural buyer.
- **US 13F exposure:** US investors mostly hold via the **DLELY ADR** (sponsored ADR; custody switched to Citi in March 2026 — routine change). Holders include BlackRock, Vanguard, Capital Group through global mandates.

Institutional read: **Bullish but crowded.** Heavy foreign ownership and large passive weights mean the stock is sensitive to any global risk-off flow out of Taiwan.

### Sentiment Divergences

- **Price vs. fundamentals (bullish-contrarian):** Stock pulled back from an all-time high of NT$2,370 on May 7 to ~NT$2,095 on May 23 (**~-12%**) despite Q1 earnings beating and guidance for +20-30% QoQ in Q2. Fundamentals accelerating while sentiment took a profit-taking pause after a ~6x 12-month rally (52-week low NT$365).
- **Crowded-trade risk (bearish-contrarian):** Foreign ownership very high, sell-side 18 Buy vs. 1 Sell, stock is consensus AI top pick. Marginal buyer is scarce; any AI capex disappointment from MSFT/META/GOOG/AMZN could trigger a sharp factor-driven sell-off.
- **Citi vs. Morgan Stanley:** Debate is no longer "is AI real" but "how concentrated is the earnings stream."

Net: **post-earnings dip + accelerating fundamentals** outweigh crowding risk on a 6-12 month horizon → **Bullish**. Confidence held to 78 because the trade is consensus and valuation is at high end of historical range.
